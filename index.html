2. Information We Collect

Microphone

HarpHero uses your microphone to detect harmonica notes during gameplay. This audio is:

  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Never recorded, stored, or transmitted
  • Only active during active gameplay sessions

Account and Authentication

HarpHero offers two ways to use the app:

  • Google Play Games — sign in using your Play Games profile. We receive a secure authentication token. No email address or password is collected or stored by us. Your Play Games display name is used to generate a username.
  • Guest account — use the app anonymously. A temporary Firebase anonymous account is created. No personal information is collected and all data stays on your device only.

We do not collect or store email addresses or passwords.

Username

When you sign in with Google Play Games for the first time, a username is automatically generated from your Play Games display name. This username is:

  • Stored in our cloud database to ensure uniqueness across all players
  • Publicly visible on in-game leaderboards

Gameplay Statistics and Progress

For Google Play Games accounts, the following is synced to our cloud database (Firebase Firestore):

  • Total songs played, play time, note accuracy stats, streaks
  • Personal best score, accuracy, and combo per song
  • Harmonica tuning calibration data (recorded frequencies per hole)

Guest accounts store all of the above locally on your device only — nothing is sent to our servers.

Leaderboards

For Google Play Games accounts, your best score for each song is posted to a global leaderboard. The following is publicly visible to all players:

  • Username
  • Score, accuracy, and best combo per song

Guest accounts do not appear on leaderboards.

Firebase Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used. It collects:

  • Device model, OS version, and app version
  • Session duration and usage patterns
  • General location (country/region, derived from IP address)

We also send custom events when you play songs:

  • song_started — song name, harp key, playback speed, practice mode
  • song_completed — song name, score, accuracy, grade, note counts, duration
  • song_quit — song name, seconds played

Users in the EU, EEA, and UK are asked for explicit consent before analytics collection begins. Outside these regions, analytics is enabled by default. You can change your preference at any time in Settings → Manage Data Consent.

Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to receive anonymous crash reports. It collects:

  • Device model, OS version, and app version
  • A stack trace describing where the crash occurred
  • No personally identifiable information is included

EU, EEA, and UK users are asked for explicit consent before crash reports are sent. Outside these regions, crash reporting is enabled by default. You can change this preference in Settings → Manage Data Consent.

Profile and Local Data

The following is stored only on your device and is never transmitted:

  • Settings (volume, sensitivity, visual preferences, audio offset)
  • Profile picture

Photo Library

If you set a profile picture, the app reads one image from your photo library. That image is stored locally as a file and is never uploaded or transmitted.

3. How We Use Your Information

5. Data Deletion

Delete Account (in Profile):

Permanently deletes your Firebase Authentication account, username, all stats, scores, tuning data, and leaderboard entries. Local device data is also cleared. This action cannot be undone.

You may also visit tgaspe.github.io/harphero-privacy/delete-account or contact us at chewpacastudios@gmail.com to request manual deletion.

6. Data Retention

  • Microphone audio — never retained
  • Firebase Analytics data — per Google's policies (typically 14 months)
  • Firebase Auth account — until account deletion
  • Cloud gameplay data (Firestore) — until account deletion
  • Local device data — until app uninstall or account deletion
  • Guest session identifier — deleted on app uninstall

7. Children's Privacy

HarpHero is intended for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us and we will delete the account and associated data.

8. Your Rights

European Economic Area and UK (GDPR)

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — delete your account in-app or contact us
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw Consent — change analytics and crash report preferences in Settings → Manage Data Consent
  • Restrict — request limits on how your data is used

California Residents (CCPA)

  • Right to Know — what personal information is collected about you
  • Right to Delete — delete your account in-app or contact us
  • Right to Opt Out of Sale — we do not sell your data

To make a request, contact us at: chewpacastudios@gmail.com

We will respond within 30–45 days.

9. Data Security

Local data is protected by the Android app sandbox. Account credentials are managed by Firebase Authentication and never stored by us directly. Cloud data (Firestore) is protected by Firebase security rules — only you can read or modify your own data. No method of data transmission is 100% secure.
