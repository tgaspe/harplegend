2. Information We Collect

2.1 Microphone Access

HarpHero requests access to your device's microphone to detect harmonica notes during active gameplay. This audio input is:

  • Processed entirely on your device using pitch detection algorithms
  • Never recorded, stored, or transmitted to us or any third party
  • Only active during gameplay sessions — not in menus, settings, or background

2.2 Analytics Data (Firebase Analytics)

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how the App is used. Firebase Analytics automatically collects:

  • Device model, OS version, and app version
  • Session duration and usage patterns
  • General location (country/region level, derived from IP address)
  • Android Advertising ID (if available and applicable)

In addition, we send the following custom events to Firebase when you interact with songs:

EventParameters Collected
song_startedSong name, harp key, playback speed, practice mode
song_completedSong name, score, accuracy, grade, note counts, duration
song_quitSong name, progress (seconds played)

This data is used to improve the App and understand which features are most valuable. It is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

2.3 Advertising Data (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display interstitial advertisements within the App. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Coarse location (derived from IP address)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

This data is used to serve ads, measure ad performance, and prevent fraud. Personalized ads may be shown depending on your consent choices (see Section 5). AdMob's data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

2.4 Profile and Local Data (On-Device Only)

The following data is stored locally on your device using Android's PlayerPrefs system and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Settings: volume levels, microphone sensitivity, vibration, visual preferences, audio offset calibration
  • Gameplay statistics: songs played, total play time, note counts, streaks, personal bests per song
  • Tuning calibration: per-hole frequency recordings for your specific harmonica (stored as JSON)
  • Profile: username and profile picture file path (if you choose to set one via your photo library)

2.5 Camera and Photo Library

If you choose to set a profile picture, the App uses the NativeGallery plugin to let you select an image from your device's photo library. The selected image:

  • Is accessed only upon your explicit action
  • Is stored as a local file path on your device
  • Is never uploaded or transmitted anywhere

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Operate the App — microphone input enables the core harmonica-learning gameplay
  • Improve the App — analytics data helps us understand which songs and features are most used and identify areas for improvement
  • Serve advertisements — AdMob data is used to display relevant ads, which fund continued development of the App
  • Personalize your experience — local data (settings, stats, tuning) is used to restore your preferences and progress between sessions
  • Ensure App quality — usage patterns help us identify bugs and crashes

We do not use your data to make automated decisions that significantly affect you, build profiles for sale to third parties, or contact you unsolicited.

5. Advertising and Consent

HarpHero shows interstitial ads powered by Google AdMob. We have integrated Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain your consent for personalized advertising where required by law (e.g., in the European Economic Area and UK).

  • If you consent to personalized ads, AdMob may use your advertising ID and other signals to show ads tailored to your interests.
  • If you do not consent (or if consent is not required in your region), non-personalized ads may still be shown.

To opt out of personalized advertising at the device level:

  1. Open your device's Settings
  2. Go to Privacy (or Google → Ads)
  3. Enable Opt out of Ads Personalization (or Delete Advertising ID)
