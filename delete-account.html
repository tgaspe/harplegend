Delete Your HarpLegend Account

ChewPacaStudios (theo_dev) · Home · Privacy Policy

You can delete your HarpLegend account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to do it directly from the app, and how to submit a manual request if needed.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your scores, statistics, and tuning calibration data will be erased.

Option 1 — Delete from the App (Recommended)

  1. Open HarpLegend on your Android device.
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Manual Deletion Request

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

Email: chewpacastudios@gmail.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request
Include: The Google Play Games display name or email address associated with your account.

We will process your request within 30 days and confirm via email once complete.
